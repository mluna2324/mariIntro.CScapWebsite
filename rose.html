Roses are my least favorite flower because of their thorns. But I guess I can put some fun facts about them up here too.

  1. Roses are well known as symbols of love
  2. Roses are used in perfumes a lot because of their nice scent
  3. It is England's country flower and the state flower of four states in the United States.
