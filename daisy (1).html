Daisies

Fun Facts

  • They are the largest family of plants in the entire world
    • The family of daisies make up almost 10% of all plants on this earth. That's incredible.
  • Bees are a huge fan of daisies
    • They are vital in the honey making process
  • You can find them on every continent except Antartica
    • And if not carefully grown and contained, they can actually turn into weeds!
  • Daisy leaves are put into salads to eat
    • They are apparently a good source of Vitamin C (I do not like salads though)
