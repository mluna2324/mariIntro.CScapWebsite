Sunflowers

Why I Love Them

Sunflowers are my personal favorite flowers. My parents used to always grow them in our backyard and they would always grow so high. The tallest sunflower that ever grew I believe was about 30 feet tall! That's taller than any human! But to get that tall they need a lot of sun, at least 6-8 hours of sun a day.
